With an SSL certificate, your website’s URL begins with "https://", which signals to visitors that the site is secure and trustworthy. Content delivery networks (CDNs) are another effective tool for improving website speed. CDNs are a network of servers distributed across various locations worldwide that deliver website content to users from the nearest server. By utilizing a CDN, websites can reduce latency and load times for users located far from the website’s primary server. CDNs can efficiently serve static assets like images, videos, and stylesheets, allowing for faster content delivery and improving the overall speed and performance of the website.
